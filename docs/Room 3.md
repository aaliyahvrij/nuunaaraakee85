## Flowers (items)

the player must examine all the flowers in order to choose the right ones

### Flame Lily (Red)

-   **Description**: The Flame Lily (_Gloriosa superba_) is a striking, _poisonous_ flower native to African and South Asian countries. Its vibrant red and yellow petals curl back dramatically, creating a flame-like appearance that makes it both beautiful and dangerous.

### Strelitzia (Yellow)

-   **Description**: Commonly known as the Bird of Paradise flower (_Strelitzia reginae_), this bright yellow bloom resembles the bird family it’s named after. Native to South Africa, its unique shape and vivid colors make it a popular ornamental plant.

## Plumeria (Pink)

-   **Description**: The Plumeria (_Plumeria rubra_), also known as frangipani, is a highly fragrant flower that exudes a delightful scent reminiscent of jasmine, citrus, and gardenia. These pink blossoms are often used in leis and are a symbol of beauty and grace in many cultures.

### Tacca Integrifolia (White/Black)

-   **Description**: Known as the White Batflower (_Tacca integrifolia_), this exotic plant features large white bracts that resemble bat wings, with long whisker-like filaments. The actual flowers are small and dark, adding a mysterious allure to this unique plant.

### Cape Primrose (Pink/White)

-   **Description**: The Cape Primrose (_Streptocarpus saxorum_) is a charming flower that blooms in shades of pink and white. Native to Africa, it is known for its delicate, trumpet-shaped flowers and is popular among gardeners for its long-lasting blooms and ease of care.

## Rainbow Roses (Fake)

-   **Description**: Rainbow Roses are not naturally occurring flowers. These vibrant and colorful roses are created through a special dyeing process that infuses each petal with different colors, resulting in a strikingly beautiful and artificial bloom.
